OTC Desk for Institutional & Corporate Clients

Peloton offers a dedicated Over-the-Counter (OTC) service, enabling legal entities to execute large-volume Fiat–Crypto–Fiat transactions with full regulatory compliance, competitive pricing, and personalized guidance from our institutional team.

This service is designed for companies that need professional, reliable access to digital asset liquidity without using standard retail channels.

What the OTC Desk Enables

Technology built for digital businesses . All Peloton services are delivered through a single, modern API. You can start with one product and add more over time — without rebuilding your integration.

01

High-value crypto purchases settled in PLN, EUR, or USD

02

Conversion of crypto to fiat with payouts directly to corporate bank accounts

03

Stablecoin liquidity for operational, treasury, or settlement needs

04

Cross-asset swaps (e.g., BTC ⇄ ETH, USDT ⇄ EUR, etc.)

05

Structured OTC deals for recurring clients or predictable volumes

Who is it for?

Web3 / blockchain companies / E-commerce platforms handling large crypto flows / Corporations using crypto for treasury, settlement, or supplier payments / Licensed financial institutions / Payment service providers / Investment firms /
